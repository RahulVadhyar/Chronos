Detailed Description

The attributes of a shape involved in animation.

The parameters of the shapes that are needed for animation are stored in this struct.

Note:

The parameters given(x, y, rotation, xSize, ySize) are relative to the parent node. They are not absolute positions.

Parameters
xThe x position of the shape.
yThe y position of the shape.
rotationThe rotation of the shape.
xSizeThe x size of the shape.
ySizeThe y size of the shape.
showWhether the shape is to be shown or not.
